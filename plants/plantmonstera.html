MONSTERA DELICIOSA

(MONSTERA DELICIOSA)

Monstera deliciosa is a member of the Araceae family and is closely related to other popular ornamental plants like philodendrons and anthuriums. In its natural habitat, it can grow into a sizable vine, climbing on trees for support.

While it is prized for its striking foliage, Monstera deliciosa also produces a distinctive fruit. The fruit, often called "Monstera fruit" or "Swiss cheese fruit," is edible when ripe and has a unique flavor, described as a blend of pineapple and banana.

However, it is essential to note that consuming unripe fruit or other parts of the plant can cause irritation, so proper ripening and preparation are necessary for safe consumption.
